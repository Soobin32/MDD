About Us

SleepyKeepy?

SleepyKeepy specializes in developing innovative solutions for monitoring children's sleep health, with a primary focus on detecting and addressing pediatric sleep apnea. Our technology helps parents track sleep patterns and vital signs, such as abdominal movement and oxygen levels, enpoling early detection of sleep-related issues.

Our Vision

We aspire to be a global leader in pediatric healthcare technology, making early diagnosis and continuous sleep monitoring accessible to families everywhere. Through Al-driven innovations, we aim to ensure that every child sleeps soundly and wakes up energized.


Our Mission

Our mission is to empower parents with real-time insights into their child's sleep health. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, we aim to provide a user-friendly solution that enables proactive healthcare interventions, improving long-term well-being for children worldwide.

Our Values

  • Empathy: Understanding parents' concerns and creating solutions that provide peace of mind.
  • Accessibility: Ensuring effortless monitoring of sleep health anytime, anywhere.
  • Reliability: Delivering accurate, real-time data for informed healthcare decisions.
  • Education: Raising awareness about pediatric sleep apnea and equipping parents with essential knowledge.
